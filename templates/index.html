About Aethera

Aethera is a cinematic, interactive sky atlas that transforms celestial geometry into layered sound. Explore constellation topology, browse myth archives, and replay sonification linked to real star data.

Feature 01 - VIRTUAL FOOTPRINT

Interactive Live Sky

Experience the firmament exactly as it appears from your coordinates. Our real-time projection engine maps RA and Dec into a fluid, interactive canvas. Tap stars to trigger unique synthesis profiles or reveal mythic geometry.

Explore the Sky
Feature 02 - NEURAL AUDIO

Sonification Library

A dedicated archive for the voice of the stars. Powered by ElevenLabs, each track is a generative blend of mythological narrative and astronomical data. Search and play high-fidelity MP3s generated by our Synthesis Engine.

Listen to Library
Feature 03 - MYTHIC ARCHIVE

Celestial Index

Dive deep into the stories that have shaped humanity's view of the night sky. Our index provides cinematic constellation cards, detailed mythic backgrounds, and featured star lists for every set of coordinates.
