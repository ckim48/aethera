User Guide

Follow these steps in order. Explore live in the Sky first, then use the Archive to read myths and replay constellation audio.

Go to Sky Open Archive
Step 01
01

Choose location + time

Inside the Sky panel, enter Lat and Lon or click Use my location. Pick a season and hour, then press Show sky.

Step 02
02

Find a constellation

Use the Finder search (top-left) to locate a constellation. Hover a result to highlight it. Click to zoom and center the shape.

Step 03
03

Play audio stars

Click a bright audio star to open the sidebar. The audio player loads automatically. Dim line-stars stay visible to preserve the constellation geometry.

Step 04
04

Open the Constellations Archive

Visit the Archive to browse constellation cards, read full myth stories, and press Play to listen again. This is the best place to explore everything in one list.
